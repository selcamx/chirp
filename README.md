Chirp is a voice dictation and meeting transcription app for macOS. Everything runs locally on your Mac — your audio never leaves your device.

## Features

**Solo Mode** — Tap a hotkey to record, release to transcribe. Text appears at your cursor instantly. Up to 190x faster than real-time on Apple Silicon.

**Meeting Mode** — Record meetings with automatic speaker identification. Chirp detects who said what, labels speakers, and builds voice profiles that persist across meetings.

**Lives in the Notch** — Always one tap away from your MacBook's notch. Never in the way.

## Privacy

| | Cloud transcription | Chirp |
|---|---|---|
| Processing | Uploaded to servers | 100% on your Mac |
| Account required | Yes | No |
| Works offline | No | Yes |
| Audio leaves device | Yes | Never |

No cloud. No accounts. No data collection. Your words stay yours.

## System Requirements

- macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later
- Apple Silicon (M1 or later)
- ~1 GB disk space for models

## Installation

Download the latest `.dmg` from [chirpvoice.com](https://chirpvoice.com/#download) or from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/selcamx/chirp/releases).

Chirp is notarized with Apple Developer ID and updates automatically via Sparkle.

## Models

Chirp downloads AI models on first launch. All models run locally:

| Model | Purpose | Size |
|-------|---------|------|
| Parakeet TDT v2 | Solo transcription (English) | ~500 MB |
| Whisper Large v3 Turbo | Meeting transcription (100 languages) | ~834 MB |
| ECAPA-TDNN | Speaker voice embeddings | ~70 MB |
| wav2vec2 | Word-level timestamps | ~380 MB |

Models are also available as [GitHub Release](https://github.com/selcamx/chirp/releases) assets.
